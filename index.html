The SIGCHI-Italy maintains a repository of courses in Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) available in Italy since 2015 and a list of the HCI Research Labs since 2019.

For both topics, you can browse an automatically-updated overview including maps and charts and you can search for particular courses or labs.

Are you interested in adding your course, lab or in updating the information of an existing entry? It's as easy as sending us a pull request!
